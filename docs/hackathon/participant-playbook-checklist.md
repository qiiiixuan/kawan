# Participant Playbook Checklist

This checklist captures event requirements from the participant playbook so the team does not optimize only for product build and miss submission logistics.

## Event

- Event: The Good Hack 2026.
- Theme: Ageing Society.
- Selected problem statement: PS2, "How might we reduce manual and time-consuming tasks within the eldercare ecosystem so that more seniors are well-supported?"
- Organisers: GoodHub SEA and Open Government Products.
- Venue: OGP Office, 51 Bras Basah Rd, #04-08 Lazada One, Singapore 189554.
- Dates: Friday 8 May to Sunday 10 May 2026.
- Team size: 4-5 participants.

## Deadlines

| Item | Deadline |
|---|---|
| Team details | Friday 8 May 2026, 11:59 PM |
| Draw manual lots for demo sequence | Saturday 9 May 2026, 5:30 PM |
| Submit solution and MVP form | Sunday 10 May 2026, 1:00 PM |
| Audience vote: Ahgong/Ahma's Favourite | Sunday 10 May 2026, 4:30 PM |
| Event feedback survey | Sunday 10 May 2026, 5:30 PM |

## Submission Requirements

The project must be:

- A coded solution. No pure no-code submission.
- Deployed and demonstrable with working infrastructure.
- Able to show working APIs through terminal, IDE, or browser during the live demo.
- Open-sourced on GitHub or equivalent.
- Beneficial to nonprofits and/or elderly communities.
- Singapore-first and SEA-scalable.

## Demo Format

- Total time per team: 10 minutes.
- Pitch: 5 minutes.
- Judges' Q&A: 5 minutes.
- Required pitch asset: slide deck exported as PDF.
- Required demo asset: working prototype with deployed infrastructure/API proof.

## Judging Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | What to show |
|---|---:|---|
| Problem-Solution Fit | 35% | Specific elderly pain point, clear triage relief, live GoodBois flow |
| Scalability Across Cultures & Borders | 15% | Multilingual/dialect support, SEA city adaptation, config-driven agency directory |
| Long-term Sustainability | 15% | B2G/pilot path, partner/funding logic, credible operating cost |
| Go-to-Market Strategy | 15% | Beachhead estate, RC/MP volunteer adoption, 8-week pilot sequence |
| Innovation & Creativity | 10% | Dialect-aware triage, allowlisted tool surface, structured handoff |
| Presentation & Storytelling | 10% | Mrs Tan story, clean demo, confident Q&A |

## Awards to Keep in Mind

- Most Impactful.
- Most Innovative Use of Technology.
- Best Cultural & Regional Scalability.
- Ahgong/Ahma's Favourite, chosen by audience vote.

## Provided Tooling

- OpenAI API credits and ChatGPT Plus access.
- Lovable credits.
- Cloudflare free-tier access to SEA-Lion.
- Google Cloud Platform credits.

The target GoodBois stack is still Cloudflare-first. Provided credits are useful for fallback, prototyping, or pitch support, but product architecture should stay aligned with `docs/system-design/tech-stack.md`.

## Pitch Must Not Forget

- Name the exact user and setting: elderly resident in an HDB void deck.
- Show that the product helps elderly residents and reduces manual triage for volunteers.
- Show the app is coded, deployed, and API-backed.
- Mention open-source continuation and GoodHub SEA community fit.
- Tie the final slide back to the judging rubric.
